About intelEcho

Built in Canada.
Built for the Future.

intelEcho was founded with a single conviction: that artificial intelligence should be accessible, actionable, and transformative for every business — not just the Fortune 500.

Headquartered in Toronto, Ontario, we combine deep machine learning expertise with a pragmatic Canadian approach to building technology that solves real problems, at scale.

From our roots in retail intelligence to a full suite of AI-driven automation products, intelEcho has grown into one of the world's leading applied AI companies — trusted by enterprises across the globe.

Our Mission

Democratizing AI for Every Canadian Business

For too long, enterprise-grade artificial intelligence has been the exclusive domain of the largest corporations with the deepest pockets. We believe that's wrong — and we're doing something about it.

intelEcho builds modular, integration-ready AI products that a 10-person ecommerce brand can deploy just as effectively as a national retail chain. Our SaaS architecture means there's no expensive infrastructure, no lengthy implementation, and no army of consultants required.

We're particularly proud of our work with Canadian retailers, helping them compete on a global stage against the largest marketplaces in the world — and win.
