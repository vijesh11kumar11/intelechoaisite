FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with compareAI.

How does billing work after I subscribe? +
Once you subscribe, your plan is activated immediately and your card is charged at the start of each billing cycle (monthly or annual). You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period — you will not be charged again after cancelling.
What ecommerce platforms does compareAI support? +
compareAI natively integrates with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and BigCommerce through one-click plugins. For platforms not listed, we provide a full REST API and webhook system that allows integration with any ecommerce stack. Our technical team can also assist with custom integrations on Enterprise plans.
Will compareAI ever lower my prices below my minimum margin? +
Never. Margin floor protection is a foundational feature of every plan. You set minimum price thresholds — either as an absolute dollar amount or as a percentage markup over your cost — and compareAI will never breach those boundaries, regardless of what competitors do. Your margins are always protected.
How quickly does compareAI detect competitor price changes? +
On the Launch plan, we run 2 price comparison scans per month across your monitored platforms. On the Grow (Professional) plan, we run 4 comparison scans per month for more frequent competitive insights. Enterprise clients receive a custom scan schedule tailored to their needs.
Where is my data stored? Is it secure? +
Yes. All data processed and stored by compareAI resides in secure, enterprise-grade data centres. intelEcho is fully compliant with applicable privacy legislation and industry-standard security practices. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit written consent.
Can I switch plans or cancel at any time? +
Yes. All plans can be upgraded, downgraded, or cancelled at any time with no penalties or lock-in periods. Plans are billed annually upfront. If you cancel, you retain access until the end of your current billing period.
What happens if I exceed my SKU limit? +
If your catalogue grows beyond your plan's SKU limit, compareAI will notify you and pause monitoring on the oldest SKUs until you either upgrade your plan or purchase an Extra SKU Pack add-on ($49 CA per 1,000 SKUs/month). We never silently drop active monitoring without alerting you first.
Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits? +
Yes. We offer a 30% startup discount for companies less than 2 years old with less than $2M in annual revenue, and a 40% non-profit discount for registered charities. Please reach out to our sales team with proof of eligibility to apply the discount to your account.
