How It Works

Four Steps to Automated Price Dominance

compareAI handles the entire pricing lifecycle — from data ingestion to automatic store updates — so you can focus on growing your business.

1

Connect Your Store

Install our native plugin for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, or connect via our REST API. Setup takes under 10 minutes with no coding required.

2

Define Your Rules

Set minimum margin floors, maximum discount thresholds, and competitor match strategies. Tell compareAI exactly how aggressive you want to be.

Prices Auto-Update

Core Features

Everything You Need to Win on Price

compareAI is built for retailers who understand that pricing is both a science and a strategy.

AI-Powered Product Matching

Our NLP and computer vision models identify the same product across different marketplaces regardless of title variations, bundles, or regional naming differences.

Real-Time Price Sync

Price changes detected on monitored platforms trigger an immediate recalculation and an automatic push to your store within 60 seconds on Professional and Enterprise plans.

Margin Protection Rules

Set absolute or percentage-based floor prices per product, category, or across your entire catalogue. compareAI will never drop a price below your defined threshold — ever.

Competitive Analytics Dashboard

Visualize your pricing position relative to each competitor, track historical price trends, and identify strategic opportunities where you can raise prices without losing conversions.

Pricing Strategies Supported

  • Match Lowest: Always match the cheapest competitor price
  • Beat by Percentage: Undercut the lowest by a fixed %
  • Beat by Amount: Undercut by a fixed dollar value
  • Price Match Only: Only update when you're higher, not lower
  • Intelligent Dynamic: AI decides the optimal price based on demand signals and competitor movement
